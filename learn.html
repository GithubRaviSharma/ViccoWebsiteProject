We are one of the leading manufacturers of LT PVC/ XLPE FRLS Power & Control Cables, Instrumentation Cables, LT Aerial Bunched Cables, AAC, ACSR, AAAC Conductors of various types, suitable for special and critical application since 1979. We have maintained quality standard as per BIS, ITD, ISO 9001:2015 etc. and are registered with NSIC and MSME.

We have a modern well-equipped laboratory with all the necessary equipment/instruments for conducting all tests as per IS – 694, IS – 1554 (Pt.-I), IS – 7098 (Pt – I), IS – 398 (Pt – I), IS – 398 (Pt – II), IS – 398 (Pt – IV), ITD, BSI, IEC, IEEE, ASTMD, MIL, IPCEA, NEMA etc.

We have a separate quality control department looking after the quality of incoming raw materials, in-process materials, and finished products. Our products have been successfully type tested at CPRI, ERDA, NTH and various other government recognized test laboratories.

We are on the approved list of the suppliers with all State Electricity Boards, Public Sector Undertakings, EPC Contractors etc and have supplied cables & conductors to almost all sectors such as Power Generation, Transmission & Distribution, Telecommunications, Railways, Petrochemical etc.
